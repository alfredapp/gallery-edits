## Usage

Get instant search suggestions from JustWatch via the `jw` keyword.

![JustWatch in-line results](images/jw.png)

* <kbd>↩</kbd> View detailed JustWatch information in Alfred.
* <kbd>⌘</kbd><kbd>↩</kbd> Open the selected title on JustWatch.
* <kbd>⇧</kbd> Preview the selected title with Quick Look.

![JustWatch streaming services](images/just-batman-wtw.png)

* <kbd>⌘</kbd><kbd>↩</kbd> Open the title in your browser.

Configure your preferred language, country, and number of results from the workflow configuration.
